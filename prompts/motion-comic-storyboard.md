# MD 法師鬥惡龍 — Motion Comic 分鏡（24 張）

> 4-Beat × 5-7 張 = 24 張關鍵幀的 motion comic 結構
> 採 Anchor + Shot 雙層 prompt 設計
> 對應策略：[`project_motion_comic_strategy`](../../.claude/projects/c--Users-USER-leaflune/memory/project_motion_comic_strategy.md)

---

## 結構說明

```
每張靜畫 prompt = Part 0（錨點、不動）+ Shot 描述（變）

Part 0 鎖：角色 + 世界 + 風格 + 鏡頭語言
Shot 描述：當下構圖 + 視角 + 動作瞬間 + 情緒

每張 ~1 秒上鏡時間（含 Ken Burns + 轉場）
24 張 ≈ 24-30 秒 motion comic 成品
```

---

## 🎬 Part 0 — 錨點（每張 prompt 都帶）

```
[CHARACTERS]
TWO WIZARDS as a team:
- BLUE WIZARD: tall, hooded deep blue robe with silver trim,
  grey beard, gnarled wooden staff topped with crackling
  sapphire-blue lightning. Aggressive stance.
- CRIMSON WIZARD: shorter, hooded scarlet/crimson robe with
  gold trim, clean-shaven, dark wooden staff, emerald-green
  circular runes from outstretched palm. Defensive stance.

DRAGON: colossal black-scaled armored dragon, glowing molten
lava cracks across body, spike crown and ridge horns, massive
bat wings, smoldering red eyes, breathes orange-white fire.

[WORLD]
Night battlefield, smoldering ruins of war banners and broken
weapons, thick ash falling, embers swirling, stormy crimson
sky with flickering storm clouds, scattered fires illuminating
the ground.

[STYLE]
Dark fantasy painterly photoreal cinematic. High contrast.
Strong rim lighting. Volumetric smoke. Cinematic depth of field.
Palette: deep blacks, crimson, ember orange, electric blue,
emerald green. NO anime, NO cartoon.
```

## 🎥 Beat 1 — Dragon's Wrath（6 張）

### 1.1 廣景：荒涼戰場、龍遠處輪廓
```
SHOT: Extreme wide shot. Vast smoldering battlefield in the
foreground with broken banners and weapons. The DRAGON's
silhouette looms distant against the crimson sky, half-hidden
in smoke. Ash drifts.
COMPOSITION: Rule of thirds, dragon top-right, ruins bottom-left.
CAMERA: Static wide, low horizon line.
```

### 1.2 鏡頭推近：龍身體浮現
```
SHOT: Medium-wide. The DRAGON's massive armored body emerges
clearly from the smoke. Lava cracks pulse along its flanks.
Wings half-folded. Tail sweeping the rubble.
COMPOSITION: Dragon fills frame center.
CAMERA: Slight low angle.
```

### 1.3 龍眼睛特寫
```
SHOT: Extreme close-up. The DRAGON's burning red eye, glowing
hot from within. Reflected ember light. Scales around the
eye crackle with molten heat.
COMPOSITION: Eye fills 60% of frame, rest dark scale texture.
CAMERA: Macro tight focus.
```

### 1.4 龍立起身軀
```
SHOT: Medium shot. The DRAGON rears up on hind legs, wings
beginning to unfurl. Chest exposed showing the brightest
lava cracks. Mouth opening.
COMPOSITION: Vertical composition, dragon dominating.
CAMERA: Low angle looking up, heroic dragon shot.
```

### 1.5 咆嘯瞬間
```
SHOT: Close-medium. The DRAGON's maw fully open, teeth gleaming,
heat distortion in the air. Embers and sparks erupt from
its throat. Sound visualized as shockwave ripples in the ash.
COMPOSITION: Mouth dead center, intense.
CAMERA: Low angle three-quarter view.
```

### 1.6 翼全展震波擴散
```
SHOT: Wide shot. The DRAGON with wings fully spread blocking
the sky, the roar's shockwave rolling outward visibly as
heat ripples and ash swirls. Background sky darkening.
COMPOSITION: Wings frame entire image, dragon centered.
CAMERA: Static wide, dramatic silhouette against red sky.
```

## 🎥 Beat 2 — Failed Synergy（6 張）

### 2.1 雙法師對峙姿態
```
SHOT: Wide shot. The TWO WIZARDS stand twenty meters apart
on the burning battlefield, facing the distant dragon (out
of frame or small in background). BLUE WIZARD on left,
staff raised aggressively. CRIMSON WIZARD on right, palm
forward defensively.
COMPOSITION: Wizards in lower third, sky/dragon presence above.
CAMERA: Ground level, behind/over wizards' shoulders.
```

### 2.2 藍袍法師詠唱閃電
```
SHOT: Medium close-up. BLUE WIZARD raising his staff with both
hands, sapphire-blue lightning crackling and gathering at
the staff's tip, light illuminating his determined face from
below.
COMPOSITION: Wizard 2/3 frame, lightning glow as key light.
CAMERA: Side angle, dynamic.
```

### 2.3 閃電擊中龍鱗、無效
```
SHOT: Close-up on the DRAGON's flank. A bright blue lightning
bolt strikes the armored scales, sparks scattering but the
scales absorb the energy with minimal damage. Tiny burn marks.
COMPOSITION: Impact point centered, lightning trail visible.
CAMERA: Tight, action moment frozen.
```

### 2.4 紅袍法師獨自開盾
```
SHOT: Medium shot. CRIMSON WIZARD has summoned a translucent
emerald-green magical shield in front of HIMSELF ONLY (not
covering his teammate). His face shows concentrated effort
but a hint of self-preservation panic.
COMPOSITION: Wizard left, shield filling right half of frame.
CAMERA: Three-quarter front view.
```

### 2.5 龍噴火覆蓋雙人
```
SHOT: Wide cinematic. The DRAGON unleashes a torrent of
orange-white fire that sweeps across both wizards. The
crimson wizard's shield is small and not enough. Fire
consumes the foreground.
COMPOSITION: Diagonal fire stream, wizards engulfed.
CAMERA: Side wide, capturing the full firestream.
```

### 2.6 兩人被火焰震退
```
SHOT: Low angle. Both wizards thrown backward by the
firestream, robes smoldering, knees buckling, staffs barely
held onto. Their faces show pain. Embers raining around them.
COMPOSITION: Both wizards in frame, dynamic backward motion.
CAMERA: Low angle, debris flying past camera.
```

## 🎥 Beat 3 — Wordless Pact（5 張）

### 3.1 藍袍法師跪地、臉頰流血
```
SHOT: Close-up. BLUE WIZARD on his knees in the burning debris,
head bowed, blood trickling from a cut on his cheek, beard
singed at the edges, staff planted in the ground beside him.
COMPOSITION: Tight on face and shoulders, shallow DoF.
CAMERA: Eye level, intimate.
```

### 3.2 緩緩轉頭
```
SHOT: Profile close-up. BLUE WIZARD's head slowly turning
toward his teammate. Time-frozen sense of motion captured in
his eyes shifting. Ember slow-motion floating in foreground.
COMPOSITION: Profile silhouette against fire glow.
CAMERA: Side profile, lit from behind.
```

### 3.3 紅袍法師喘息接到眼神
```
SHOT: Close-up. CRIMSON WIZARD kneeling, panting heavily,
sweat on his forehead, looking up to meet his teammate's
gaze. His face shows recognition — they understand each
other.
COMPOSITION: Tight on face, slight upward look.
CAMERA: Eye level, slightly higher than subject.
```

### 3.4 兩人眼神相對
```
SHOT: Two-shot extreme close-up. Both wizards' eyes locked
on each other across the frame, divided by a blurred
foreground of floating embers. Time stops. A heartbeat
moment. Pact crystallizing wordlessly.
COMPOSITION: Split frame, eye-to-eye, embers between.
CAMERA: Profile two-shot, shallow DoF on eyes.
```

### 3.5 藍袍輕微點頭、紅袍握緊法杖
```
SHOT: Detail shot. BLUE WIZARD's slight nod captured mid-motion,
CRIMSON WIZARD's gloved knuckles whitening as he tightens
grip on his staff. Embers floating around. Quiet resolve.
COMPOSITION: Two-shot tight, hands and faces visible.
CAMERA: Low medium angle, intimate.
```

## 🎥 Beat 4 — Double Shield Reversal（7 張）

### 4.1 雙人同步起身
```
SHOT: Wide shot. Both wizards rise simultaneously from kneeling,
robes billowing, staffs lifted in synchronized motion. Twin
auras beginning to gather. The battlefield around them lit
by their rising magic.
COMPOSITION: Both wizards centered, low horizon.
CAMERA: Low angle, heroic shot.
```

### 4.2 法杖同時頓地
```
SHOT: Medium shot. Both staffs slam into the ground in perfect
unison, sending up dust and embers. Shockwave radiating
outward from the impact points. Faces grim with resolve.
COMPOSITION: Both staff bases visible, impact wave from each.
CAMERA: Low angle, dramatic.
```

### 4.3 雙盾迸發
```
SHOT: Split close-up showing both wizards. BLUE WIZARD's
sapphire shield bursts forward in geometric crystalline
patterns. CRIMSON WIZARD's emerald shield mirrors it on
the other side. Both shields growing.
COMPOSITION: Diptych composition, mirrored magic.
CAMERA: Symmetric two-shot.
```

### 4.4 雙盾融合成巨大光罩
```
SHOT: Wide shot. The two shields expand and fuse into one
colossal dome of crackling sapphire-emerald light,
encompassing both wizards. The dome shimmers with magical
energy. Lightning arcs across its surface.
COMPOSITION: Dome dominates frame, wizards small within.
CAMERA: Wide low angle, awe-inspiring.
```

### 4.5 龍噴火撞上光罩
```
SHOT: Close-up on the dome's surface. The DRAGON's torrent of
orange-white fire crashes against the shield dome, momentarily
held, the magical surface bowing inward, light intensifying,
about to release.
COMPOSITION: Wall of fire meeting wall of magic, centered.
CAMERA: Tight on impact point, dramatic.
```

### 4.6 火焰反彈成白熱光柱
```
SHOT: Wide cinematic. The reflected energy converges and
launches backward from the dome as a concentrated lance of
white-hot magical light, streaking toward the dragon.
Slow-motion intense moment.
COMPOSITION: Lance of light diagonal across frame from dome
to dragon.
CAMERA: Side wide, capturing the whole trajectory.
```

### 4.7 龍倒地、碎甲飛濺
```
SHOT: Wide shot. The DRAGON struck in the chest by the lance,
roaring in agony, scales shattering and falling away,
collapsing backward in a shower of embers and broken armor.
The wizards stand victorious in the foreground.
COMPOSITION: Dragon center-back, wizards small foreground.
CAMERA: Three-quarter wide, victorious.
```

## 📊 製作節奏摘要

```
總計：24 張關鍵幀
─ Beat 1（龍）：6 張
─ Beat 2（失敗）：6 張
─ Beat 3（默契）：5 張
─ Beat 4（反殺）：7 張

成片長度估計：
─ 平均每張 1 秒上鏡（含 Ken Burns）
─ 加轉場 + 字幕 → 25-30 秒成片

生成時間（Flux Schnell on home 6GB）：
─ ~15 秒 / 張 × 24 張 = ~6 分鐘
─ + self-review + 重生 = 共 ~10-15 分鐘
```

## 📐 影片規格（成片）

```
比例：16:9（YouTube 主版本）
解析度：1920×1080（從 1024×576 圖片 upscale 或直接生 1080p）
幀率：24 fps（電影感、配合靜畫氣質）
長度：25-30 秒

短影音版本：
─ Reframe 為 9:16、適合 IG Reels / TikTok / YT Shorts
─ 可砍掉一些廣景、保留人物特寫
```

## 🎵 後製方向（給未來剪輯參考）

```
轉場節奏：
─ Beat 1（龍咆嘯）：cross-fade + zoom-in、神祕到震撼
─ Beat 2（失敗）：cut + 翻頁感、快速失敗節奏
─ Beat 3（默契）：slow dissolve、慢動作呼吸
─ Beat 4（反殺）：accelerating cut + zoom-out、史詩高潮

音效層次：
─ 環境：低頻風聲、火焰嗶剝聲
─ SFX：龍吼、雷擊、火噴、盾爆、法杖頓地、玻璃碎裂感
─ BGM：dark fantasy 弦樂、Beat 3 安靜、Beat 4 漸強到爆發

字幕（選用）：
─ Beat 開頭可加 chapter card：「I. 惡龍降臨」「II. 失措」「III. 心契」「IV. 反殺」
```
